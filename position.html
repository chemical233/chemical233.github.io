    We are seeking postdoctoral researchers and PhD candidates in advanced materials and technologies for all-solid-state batteries

      Qualifications:

    1. Ph.D. or equivalent degree in materials science, chemistry, physics, or a related field for Postdoctoral positions
    2. Bachelor’s or Master's degree in materials science, chemistry, physics, or a related field for Ph.D. positions
    3. Proven track record of academic excellence
    4. Strong background in materials science, chemistry, physics, or a related field
    5. Excellent written and oral communication skills in English

      Responsibilities:

    1. Conduct research on advanced materials and technologies for all-solid-state batteries
    2. Publish high-quality research papers in top-tier journals
    3. Participate in grant proposal writing and scientific presentations
    4. Train and mentor graduate and undergraduate students

      Salary and Benefits:

    1. Competitive salary and benefits package
    2. Access to world-class facilities and expertise
    3. Opportunities for international collaborations and networking

    Application Procedure:

        Interested candidates are encouraged to submit their CV, a brief research statement, and the contact information for at least two references to changhongwang@eias.ac.cn. Shortlisted candidates will be contacted for an interview.

    Application Deadline:

    The position will remain open until filled.
        We welcome all qualified applicants regardless of their race, ethnicity, gender, or sexual orientation. We are committed to promoting diversity and equal opportunity in our institution.
